Gamepad 1:
    Drive:
        Left Joystick Y: Left Drive System
        Right Joystick Y: Right Drive System
    Lift:
        Left Trigger: Raise Lift
        Right Trigger: Lower Lift
    Depositor:
        D Pad: Raise/Lower Arms
        B: Release Bucket Latches
    Collector:
        Left Bumper: Collect
        Right Bumper: Push
        A: Raise/Lower Arm
        B: Fold/Lower Arm
